The White Dragon Horse is actually a dragon prince who was punished by Heaven and turned into a horse. He serves as Tang Sanzang's loyal steed throughout the journey. Despite his past misdeeds, the dragon redeems himself through his faithful service to the monk. His ability to transform into a dragon makes him a valuable asset on the perilous journey.
