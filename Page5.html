The White Dragon Horse is the son of Ao Run, the Dragon King of the West Sea. Banished for accidentally destroying a pearl, he was saved by Guanyin and awaited Tang Sanzang at a stream. After mistakenly eating Tang Sanzang's horse, he transforms into the White Dragon Horse and serves as the monk's loyal steed throughout the journey. At the end, he is ordained as the Great Strength Bodhisattva of the Eight Heavenly Sections.
