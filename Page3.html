Zhu Bajie, also known as Pigsy, is a former immortal who was banished to the mortal world for misconduct in Heaven. Though his gluttony, laziness, and fondness for women often lead to trouble, he is a skilled fighter and a loyal companion to Tang Sanzang. Despite his flaws, Zhu Bajie learns valuable lessons during the journey and ultimately earns a place in the afterlife, though not as highly revered as his companions.
