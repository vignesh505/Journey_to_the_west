Tang Sanzang is a devout monk chosen to undertake the sacred pilgrimage to retrieve Buddhist scriptures from the West. Born with a tragic past, he was destined for greatness as the reincarnation of Jin Chanzi, a disciple of Buddha. Though physically weak, his purity, wisdom, and unwavering compassion make him a moral guide for his companions. His quest symbolizes the spiritual journey to enlightenment, and along the way, he teaches his companions valuable lessons about virtue, patience, and self-control.
