Tang Sanzang is the reincarnation of Jin Chanzi and the central figure of the Journey to the West. His childhood was filled with calamity, and he was chosen to embark on a sacred journey to retrieve Buddhist scriptures. Throughout his journey, he is accompanied by his three disciples and the White Dragon Horse. Despite his hardships, Tang Sanzang remains a symbol of compassion and determination.
