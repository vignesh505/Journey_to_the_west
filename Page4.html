Sha Wujing, also known as Sandy, is the calm and loyal disciple of Tang Sanzang. Once a celestial general, he was punished for breaking a crystal goblet in Heaven and banished to the mortal world. Despite his terrifying appearance, he is kind-hearted and plays an important role in the journey as a peacemaker among his more fiery companions. His resilience and loyalty make him a valued member of the group.
